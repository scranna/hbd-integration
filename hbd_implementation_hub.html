Training rollout
Implementors deliver · Corporate supports · Live sessions with recordings · One session per wave, open to all BUs · Apr–Oct 2025
Approach
Format
Live sessions delivered by implementors, recorded for on-demand reference. Recordings shared with all BUs within 48hrs of each session.
Attendance
One session per wave, open to all BUs — not just the wave going live. Cross-BU attendance builds camaraderie and lets stable BUs share experience with newer ones.
Content
Same core content per system, delivered in separate sessions aligned to each wave's go-live. Implementors lead, corporate supports with HBD-specific context and process nuances.

HBD Lead: Mike / Tom · Implementor delivers
Wave 1 NS training
Jun · Audience: BluShark (+ all BUs welcome)
Core NS workflow, chart of accounts, multi-entity setup. BluShark goes live Jul 1.
Wave 2 NS training
Jul · Audience: Hennessey, CJ (+ all BUs)
Same core content. BluShark operators available as peer resource — invite them to attend and contribute.

Tip: Run a 30-min "NS tips from the field" segment at end of W2 and W3 sessions, led by the most recently live BU.
HubSpot — CRM
HBD Lead: Coulsen / Kathleen · Implementor delivers
Wave 1 HS training
Jun · Audience: BluShark (+ all BUs welcome)
AM workflow in HubSpot, deal stages, contract management. Emphasize: AMs work in HubSpot only — contracts push to NetSuite automatically.
Wave 2 HS training
Jul · Audience: Hennessey, CJ (+ all BUs)
Same core content. Include real examples from BluShark's first weeks live if available.
Wave 3 HS training
Aug · Audience: TNT, JD, Ion (+ all BUs)
Final HS training wave. By this point 2 waves live — invite early adopters to share what's working.
Tip: The variable invoicing / PPC flow (NS → HS) should be covered explicitly in every session so AMs understand what they will and won't see in HubSpot.
